Hey, I’m BINGHUAice — a tinkerer who likes to flash routers and make them do things they were never supposed to do.

What I Do

I mainly mess around with:

  • OpenWrt / ImmortalWrt / iStoreOS — custom firmware builds for various routers
  • Padavan — modified firmware for MTK-based routers, currently working on 360 T6M support
  • U-Boot (MT7621) — custom bootloader for MTK SoC platforms
  • Router hardware mods — like the WR1200JS 32MB flash upgrade

Featured Projects

  • padavan-4.4 — Padavan firmware with 360 T6M support
  • uboot-mt7621 — Customized U-Boot for MT7621
  • istoreos — iStoreOS build for WR1200JS
  • immortalwrt — ImmortalWrt for 32MB WR1200JS
  • Actions-OpenWrt — GitHub Actions CI for OpenWrt builds
